Understanding Your Mood Insights:

Average Sentiment Score: This score reflects your overall mood based on your journal entries. A positive average score means you’re generally feeling good, while a negative average score suggests you might be experiencing more negative emotions. For example, an average score of 0.75 indicates a generally positive mood.

Mood Variance: This metric shows how much your mood fluctuates. A high variance means your emotions are varying significantly, indicating possible mood swings. A low variance means your mood is stable and consistent. For example, a variance of 0.2 suggests your mood doesn’t fluctuate much, indicating stability.
